# HealthSense AI

## What is this?

A smart symptom checker that talks to you like a real doctor would. It asks one question at a time and each question depends on your previous answer - not some pre-written list.

After plenty of questions, you get a health report with possible conditions, home remedies, medicines you can buy, and when to actually visit a doctor.

## Why we built it

We were tired of:
- googling symptoms at 3 AM and convinced we have something serious
- filling out those long forms at clinics with 20 questions that don't even apply to what you're feeling

This is different because the AI actually thinks about what you say before asking the next question.

## What makes it different

Traditional symptom checkers show everyone the same questions. We don't do that. If you say "headache", we won't ask about rashes. We'll ask about duration, severity, whether light hurts your eyes - relevant stuff.
